Saturation Studio

Upload a CSV of daily ad spend + revenue. Get a Hill saturation curve, optimal-spend recommendations across LTV horizons, and a scenario-modeling chart. Runs entirely in your browser — no data leaves your machine.

What this does

  • Fits a Hill saturation curve to your daily spend → revenue data: revenue = V_max · s^h / (K^h + s^h)
  • Computes optimal daily spend for six different goals — including 3-month, 6-month, and 12-month LTV-extended contribution margin
  • Lets you tune variable cost %, NC share, LTV multipliers, adstock decay, and recency weighting live
  • Shows a spend ladder with monthly projections and a chart overlaying revenue, ROAS, marginal ROAS, breakeven, today's spend, and the selected goal optimum

CSV format
