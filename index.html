What it does

  • Fits a Hill saturation curve to your daily spend → revenue: r = V·s^h / (K^h + s^h)
  • Computes optimal daily spend for seven goals — including 3m, 6m, and 12m LTV-extended CM
  • Tunes VC %, NC share, LTV multipliers, adstock decay, target ROAS, and recency weighting live
  • Per-month curves with seasonality fallback so November ≠ February
